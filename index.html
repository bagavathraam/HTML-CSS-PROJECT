our story



Umbaui celebrates the art of making. We partner with
skilled artisans around the world to bring you textiles that
honor heritage, craftsmanship, and the beauty of
imperfection. Each piece is thoughtfully sourced, carrying
with it the story of the hands that made it. We work
exclusively with natural fibers. These materials are renewable,
biodegradable, and gentle on the earth.
